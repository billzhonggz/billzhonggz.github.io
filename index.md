# Bio

Mr. Junru ZHONG (钟钧儒) is a research assistant at Department of Imaging and Interventional Radiology, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). Before that, he was working as an assistant instructor at Division of Science and Technology (DST) of Beijing Normal University - Hong Kong Baptist University United International College (UIC) in Zhuhai, China.

He was graduated from his master degree at Department of Information Engineering of CUHK in 2020, and bachelor degree in computer science and technology at DST of UIC in 2018.

# Projects

Junru is actively working on projects involve data science, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning (ML) including deep learning (DL). He is now working on *medical imaging*, developing and implementing algorithms to detect tissues from Magnetic Resonance (MR) Images. Before that, he done projects on scene images manipulation with generative adversarial network (GAN), natural language parser building, embedding systems, and software engineering.

Research Interests

Author: Junru Zhong Date: February 9, 2020  21:41:29 Category: Research

Last updated in January, 2020.

These interests may NOT necessary to be research interests. Yet I did not take a proper research degree, and these interests come from my daily life, experiences and observations. They can be “the issues that I pay attention to”.

Yes, I don’t have much outcomes on them right now. But you know, I take really serious on them and they are the directions I am/will be working on.

An Abstract

My daily focuses lie to three major fields. Computer technologies and their applications, photography and content creating, public transportation and public good. Currently, I already purchased a bachelor degree in computer science, and working on a master degree also on this field. And I am looking for chances to work on interdisciplinary topics between computer science and public good (services), especially on transportation, urban issues, etc.

I have decided to leave photography as my leisure.

Background

I built these three interests from my childhood, and refine them continuously.

The first interest I developed is computer technology. Since my high school age, I am training myself to be a professional computer scientist by readings and higher educations. Till now, I was well trained to be a software engineer and being training on data science.

I also learned basic photography at the student club of my high school.
